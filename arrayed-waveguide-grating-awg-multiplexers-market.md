<p><p>Arrayed Waveguide Grating (AWG) Multiplexers are commonly classified into two types - Thermal AWG and Athermal AWG. Thermal AWGs require precise temperature control to maintain optimal performance, making them more costly to operate. On the other hand, Athermal AWGs are designed to operate without temperature control, offering a more cost-effective and stable solution. Both types of AWG multiplexers play a crucial role in wavelength division multiplexing systems, providing efficient and reliable signal processing for telecommunications and data transmission applications.</p></p>
