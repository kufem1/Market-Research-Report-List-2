<p><p>The Kidney Blood Purification Equipment market consists of three main types: Continuous Blood Purification (CBP), Hemodialysis (HD), and other markets. CBP involves the continuous removal of waste products and excess fluids from the blood, providing more gentle and efficient treatment. HD, on the other hand, involves removing waste products from the blood using a machine outside the body. The "other" market includes alternative blood purification methods such as peritoneal dialysis and hemofiltration. Each type offers unique benefits and caters to different patient needs.</p></p>
