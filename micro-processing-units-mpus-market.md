<p><p>Micro Processing Units (MPUs) are key components in electronic devices. The market for MPUs can be divided into two main types: Read-Only MPUs and Read/Write MPUs. Read-Only MPUs are designed for devices where data only needs to be read, such as in simple calculators. On the other hand, Read/Write MPUs are used in devices where data needs to be both read and written, such as in computers. Both types of MPUs play a crucial role in facilitating fast and efficient data processing in a wide range of electronic devices.</p></p>
