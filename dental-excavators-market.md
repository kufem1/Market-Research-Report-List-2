<p><p>Dental excavators are instruments used during dental procedures to remove decayed or damaged tooth material. There are two main types of dental excavators: round excavators and spoon excavators. Round excavators have a rounded tip, making them ideal for removing soft dentin and to create smooth cavity walls. Spoon excavators have a spoon-shaped tip, allowing for more precise excavation of tooth structure. Both types are essential tools in dentistry for effective and efficient removal of diseased tissue from teeth.</p></p>
