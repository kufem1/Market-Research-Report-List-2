<p><strong>The Automatic Nucleic Acid Extractor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impeticbead type</li><li>Straw type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic nucleic acid extractors are available in different types based on the method of extraction. Impeticbead type extractors use magnetic beads to bind to nucleic acids, allowing for easy purification. On the other hand, straw type extractors use a straw-like material to collect the nucleic acids for isolation. Both types offer efficient and reliable extraction of nucleic acids, but the choice between the two depends on the specific requirements of the laboratory and the desired level of automation.</p></p>
