<p><ul><li>Drum-Type Rotary Air Preheater</li><li>Windshield Rotary Air Preheater</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drum-Type Rotary Air Preheaters are designed with a rotating drum that allows for efficient heat transfer by increasing the contact surface area between the hot flue gas and the incoming air. On the other hand, Windshield Rotary Air Preheaters use a series of parallel tubes to heat the incoming air, providing high efficiency in a compact design. Both types of Rotary Air Preheaters are commonly used in power plants and industrial processes to improve overall energy efficiency and reduce fuel consumption.</p></p>
