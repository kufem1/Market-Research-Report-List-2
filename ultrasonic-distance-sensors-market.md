<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Distance Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic distance sensors have two main market types: Analog Type and Digital Type. Analog Type sensors provide continuous output signal proportional to the distance measured, allowing for precise distance readings. On the other hand, Digital Type sensors provide a discrete output signal, making them ideal for distance detection in binary terms. Both types of sensors offer advantages depending on the application requirements, with Analog Type sensors being more suitable for accurate measurements and Digital Type sensors being more suitable for simple detection.</p></p>
