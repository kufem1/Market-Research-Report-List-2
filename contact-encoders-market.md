<p><strong>The Contact Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Conductive Zone</li><li>Contact Insulated Zone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Contact encoders consist of two main types: contact conductive zone and contact insulated zone. In the contact conductive zone market, the encoder utilizes a conductive surface that makes physical contact with the sensor, allowing for the detection of movement. On the other hand, the contact insulated zone market involves an insulated surface that prevents direct contact between the sensor and the encoder, allowing for more precise and accurate readings. Each type caters to different applications and industries based on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
