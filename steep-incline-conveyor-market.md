<p><p>Steep incline conveyors are specially designed to transport materials up steep slopes, increasing efficiency and saving space in industrial settings. Two main types are steep slope belt conveyors and steep slope chain conveyors. Steep slope belt conveyors use a continuous loop of material to transport goods, while steep slope chain conveyors use a chain-driven mechanism. Both types are commonly used in industries requiring vertical transportation of goods such as mining, agriculture, and construction.</p></p>
