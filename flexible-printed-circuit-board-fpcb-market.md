Single Sided Flex Circuits have conductors on one side, while Double Sided Flex Circuits have conductors on both sides. Multi-Layer Flex Circuits have multiple conductor layers. Rigid Flex Circuits combine flexible and rigid substrates. Other types may include sculptured flex circuits or heavy copper flex circuits. Each type caters to different applications and requirements in various industries.</p></p>
