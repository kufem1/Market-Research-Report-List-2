<p><p>IoT modules market consists of various types such as LPWANs, Cellular (3G/4G/5G), Zigbee, Bluetooth and BLE, Wi-Fi, and RFID. LPWANs offer long-range connectivity at low power, ideal for applications like smart cities. Cellular modules provide connectivity through 3G/4G/5G networks for high-speed data transfer. Zigbee modules are commonly used for home automation and industrial applications. Bluetooth and BLE modules are popular for short-range communication. Wi-Fi modules offer high-speed connectivity for IoT devices. RFID modules enable wireless identification and tracking of objects.</p></p>
