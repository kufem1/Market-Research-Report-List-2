<p><p>Enclosed switches with a plunger design are commonly used in applications where a button or lever needs to be pushed to activate a signal or function. These switches are widely used in industrial machinery, automotive controls, and household appliances. On the other hand, panel mount switches are designed to be mounted onto a flat panel, making them easy to integrate into control panels or equipment. They are often used in control rooms, instrumentation panels, and electrical enclosures. Both types of switches offer reliable and durable performance in various settings.</p></p>
