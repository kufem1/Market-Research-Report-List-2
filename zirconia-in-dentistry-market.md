<p><p>Zirconia in dentistry is categorized into three market types based on their level of transparency. Low transparency zirconia is ideal for crowns and bridges due to its strength and durability. Medium transparency zirconia provides a balance between aesthetics and strength, making it suitable for both anterior and posterior restorations. High transparency zirconia offers the best aesthetic results and is often used for veneers and other cosmetic restorations. Dentists can choose the appropriate type of zirconia based on the specific requirements of the patient's dental restoration.</p></p>
