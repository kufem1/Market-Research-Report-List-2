<p><strong>The Truck Telematics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plug and Play Telematics</li><li>Hardwired Install Telematics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Truck telematics market includes two main types: Plug and Play Telematics and Hardwired Install Telematics. Plug and Play devices are easy to install and can be plugged directly into the vehicle's OBD-II port, offering quick and simple set-up. On the other hand, Hardwired Install Telematics involve a more permanent installation process where the device is wired directly into the vehicle's electrical system. While Plug and Play telematics are more convenient for temporary solutions, Hardwired Install Telematics offer a more secure and reliable long-term solution.</p></p>
