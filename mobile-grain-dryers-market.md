<p><p>Mobile grain dryers are essential equipment used in the agricultural industry to dry grains efficiently before storage. There are three main types of mobile grain dryers: mounted, self-propelled, and trailed. Mounted dryers are attached to a truck or tractor for transportation. Self-propelled dryers have their own propulsion system for easy movement around the farm. Trailed dryers are towed behind a tractor or truck. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of mobility, capacity, and efficiency, catering to different needs of farmers and agricultural businesses.</p></p>
