<p><p>The noninvasive glucose monitor market can be categorized into two main types: wearable and non-wearable monitors. Wearable monitors are designed to be worn on the body, providing continuous glucose monitoring without the need for frequent finger pricks. Non-wearable monitors, on the other hand, are usually handheld devices that can measure glucose levels through alternative methods such as saliva or tear samples. Both types of monitors aim to provide convenient and pain-free solutions for individuals with diabetes to manage their blood sugar levels effectively.</p></p>
