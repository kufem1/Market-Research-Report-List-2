<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Thyrectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar Thyrectors</li><li>Bi-polar Thyrectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thyrectors market includes two main types: Uni-polar Thyrectors and Bi-polar Thyrectors. Uni-polar Thyrectors conduct current in one direction only and are commonly used for protecting against overvoltage in electronic circuits. In contrast, Bi-polar Thyrectors can conduct current in both directions and are often utilized in more complex applications where bidirectional protection is required. Both types play a crucial role in safeguarding electronics from voltage surges and ensuring the smooth operation of electrical systems.</p></p>
