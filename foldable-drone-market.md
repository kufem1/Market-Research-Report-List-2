<p><ul><li>Brushed Motor</li><li>Brushless Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foldable drones are categorized based on the type of motors they use: brushed or brushless. Brushed motor drones are generally cheaper and suitable for beginners, but they have limited lifespan and are less efficient. On the other hand, brushless motor drones are more expensive, but offer better performance, longer flight times, and higher durability. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences, with brushed motor drones being more budget-friendly options for casual users, while brushless motor drones are preferred by enthusiasts and professionals for their advanced features and capabilities.</p></p>
