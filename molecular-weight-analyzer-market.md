<p><p>Molecular Weight Analyzer market includes various types such as Gel Permeation Chromatography (GPC), Gas Chromatography & Mass Spec (GC/MS), Size Exclusion Chromatography (SEC), High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC), Differential Refractive Index (DRI), and Multi-Angle Laser Light Scattering (MALLS). GPC is used for determining the distribution of molecular weights in a polymer sample. GC/MS is used for separating and identifying different compounds in a mixture. SEC separates molecules based on their size. HPLC separates and quantifies compounds in a liquid mixture. DRI and MALLS are used for detecting and quantifying polymers and macromolecules.</p></p>
