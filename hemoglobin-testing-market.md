<p><p>Hemoglobin testing market includes various types such as chromatography and immunoassay. Chromatography is a technique that separates components in a mixture based on their different affinities for a stationary phase, allowing for accurate quantification of hemoglobin levels. Immunoassay, on the other hand, utilizes antibodies to detect and measure specific proteins, providing a sensitive and specific method for hemoglobin testing. Both types of testing play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring various medical conditions related to hemoglobin levels.</p></p>
