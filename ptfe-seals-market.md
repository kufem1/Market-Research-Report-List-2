<p><p>PTFE Seals market is categorized into different types based on their functionality and shape. Rod seals are used to prevent leakage between a static and moving part in a hydraulic cylinder. O Rings are circular seals used in a wide range of applications to prevent leakage of fluids. Backup Rings are used in conjunction with O Rings to provide additional support and security. Other types of PTFE seals include custom seals designed for specific applications that do not fit into the standard categories.</p></p>
