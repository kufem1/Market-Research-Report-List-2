<p><p>Electric lawn mowers come in two main types: cordless electric lawn mowers and corded electric lawn mowers. Cordless electric lawn mowers are powered by rechargeable batteries, offering freedom of movement without being constrained by a power cord. On the other hand, corded electric lawn mowers need to be plugged into a power outlet while in use, limiting the range of movement but ensuring a continuous power source. Both types offer eco-friendly and efficient alternatives to traditional gas-powered lawn mowers.</p></p>
