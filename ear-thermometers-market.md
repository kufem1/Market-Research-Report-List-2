<p><p>Ear thermometers are available in two main types - non-contact and contact. Non-contact ear thermometers use infrared technology to measure the temperature without physically touching the ear canal, making them more hygienic and convenient for use. On the other hand, contact ear thermometers require placing the probe inside the ear canal to get an accurate reading. Both types are popular in the market, with non-contact thermometers being preferred for their ease of use and contact thermometers for their accuracy.</p></p>
