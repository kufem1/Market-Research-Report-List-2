<p><p>The Vanilla Extracts market offers two main types: Liquid and Powder forms. Liquid vanilla extract is made by macerating vanilla beans in alcohol, resulting in a concentrated liquid flavoring. It is commonly used in baking and cooking. Powdered vanilla extract is made by drying and grinding vanilla beans into a fine powder. This form is often used in dry mixes or products where a liquid form may not be preferable. Both liquid and powdered vanilla extracts provide a convenient way to add the rich flavor of vanilla to various dishes.</p></p>
