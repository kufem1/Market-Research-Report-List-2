<p><p>High speed image sensor market can be categorized into two main types: digital and analog. Digital image sensors convert light into digital signals, allowing for precise image capture and processing. They are commonly used in high-end cameras and smartphones. On the other hand, analog image sensors directly capture and output electrical signals corresponding to light intensity. They are often used in industrial applications requiring real-time image processing. Both digital and analog image sensors play crucial roles in various industries requiring high speed and high-quality image capture capabilities.</p></p>
