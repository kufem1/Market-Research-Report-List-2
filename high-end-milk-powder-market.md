<p><p>High end milk powder market includes two main types: Whole Milk Powder and Skim Milk Powder. Whole Milk Powder is derived from fresh milk and contains around 26% milk fat, making it richer and creamier in taste. Skim Milk Powder, on the other hand, is made by removing the fat from whole milk, resulting in a lower fat content product. Both types of milk powder are used in various food and beverage applications, especially in high-end culinary and baking industries.</p></p>
