<p><strong>The Thyristors Triacs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SCR</li><li>GTO</li><li>IGCTs</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thyristors, Triacs and other similar devices have various types in the market including SCR (Silicon Controlled Rectifier), GTO (Gate Turn-Off Thyristor), IGCTs (Integrated Gate-Commutated Thyristors), and others. SCR is a four-layer solid-state semiconductor device that controls current flow. GTO is a type of thyristor that can be turned off by applying a negative voltage to its gate terminal. IGCTs are similar to GTOs but have higher power handling capabilities. Other types of thyristors include MCTs (MOS-Controlled Thyristor) and ETOs (Emitter Turn-Off Thyristor).</p></p>
