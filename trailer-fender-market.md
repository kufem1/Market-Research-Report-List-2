<p><p>Trailer fenders come in two main types - half fenders and full fenders. Half fenders cover only a portion of the wheels, providing basic protection against road debris while also allowing easier access for maintenance. Full fenders, on the other hand, cover the entire wheel and offer more comprehensive protection, shielding the trailer from dirt, debris, and water. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the trailer fender market, providing options for varying levels of coverage and functionality.</p></p>
