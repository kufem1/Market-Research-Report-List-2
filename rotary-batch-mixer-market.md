<p><p>Rotary Batch Mixers are commonly categorized into three types based on the number of shafts: Single-Shaft Mixer, Twin-Shaft Mixer, and 3-Shaft Mixer. Single-Shaft Mixers are known for their simple design and efficient mixing of dry powders and granules. Twin-Shaft Mixers feature two mixing shafts that work together to achieve a more thorough and uniform mixing process. 3-Shaft Mixers have an additional shaft that allows for even greater mixing precision and consistency, making them ideal for more complex mixing applications.</p></p>
