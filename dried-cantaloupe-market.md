<p><p>Dried cantaloupe is available in various market types such as baked dried and freeze dried. Freeze dried cantaloupe undergoes a process where the fruit is rapidly frozen and then placed in a vacuum to remove the moisture, resulting in a light and crispy texture while preserving the fruit's natural flavor and nutrients.
