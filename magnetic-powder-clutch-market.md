<p><ul><li>Dual Shaft Magnetic Powder Clutch</li><li>Single Shaft Magnetic Powder Clutch</li><li>Hollow Shaft Magnetic Powder Clutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic powder clutch market includes different types based on shaft designs. Dual shaft magnetic powder clutch is used when the power needs to be transmitted to two different shafts simultaneously. Single shaft magnetic powder clutch is used when power is needed to be transferred to only one shaft. Hollow shaft magnetic powder clutch is used when the input and output shafts need to be concentric. Each type caters to specific engineering requirements making them suitable for various applications in industries like automotive, printing, and packaging.</p></p>
