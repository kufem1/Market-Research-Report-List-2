<p><ul><li>Coreless Closed Loop Current Sensor</li><li>Coreless Open Loop Current Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coreless Closed Loop Current Sensors are designed with a conductive path to measure the current flow without any magnetic core. They provide accurate and reliable measurement of current without the need for any external components. On the other hand, Coreless Open Loop Current Sensors are also without a magnetic core but are designed to provide an output signal proportional to the current passing through the conductor. These sensors offer high accuracy and stability, making them suitable for various industrial applications where precise current measurement is essential.</p></p>
