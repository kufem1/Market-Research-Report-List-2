<p><p>An Optical Coupler (OC) is a device used in fiber optic communication systems to split or combine optical signals. <p><p>Optical Couplers (OC) can be classified into two main types based on their behavior: Non-linear and Linear market types. Non-linear optical couplers exhibit non-linear responses to input signals, allowing for complex modulation and signal processing. On the other hand, Linear optical couplers have a linear response to input signals, making them ideal for simple signal amplification and distribution applications. Both types play crucial roles in enabling efficient and reliable optical communication systems.</p></p>
