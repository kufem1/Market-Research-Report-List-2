<p><p>Wet blasting equipment is classified into two main types: slurry and vapor. Slurry wet blasting involves mixing water with abrasive media to create a slurry that is used for surface treatment. On the other hand, vapor wet blasting utilizes a high-pressure water vapor stream mixed with abrasive media for cleaning and finishing surfaces. Both types of wet blasting equipment are commonly used in industrial settings for tasks such as removing paint, rust, and coatings from various surfaces.</p></p>
