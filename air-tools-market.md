<p><p>Air tools market can be segmented into four main types: pneumatic cylinders, pneumatic valves, air treatment components, and auxiliary components. </p><p>Pneumatic cylinders are devices that use compressed air to create linear motion. Pneumatic valves control the flow of compressed air in a system. Air treatment components ensure that the air used in the tools is clean and dry. Auxiliary components provide additional functions such as pressure control and filtration. These different types of air tools play a crucial role in various industries for tasks such as drilling, cutting, and assembly.</p></p>
