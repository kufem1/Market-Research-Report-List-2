<p><p>PET bottle recycling market can be divided into two types: chemical and mechanical recycling. Chemical recycling involves breaking down the PET bottles into their basic chemical components through processes like depolymerization. On the other hand, mechanical recycling involves shredding and melting the PET bottles to create new products. Both markets aim to reduce waste and promote sustainability by giving a second life to the plastic bottles. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, catering to different needs in the recycling industry.</p></p>
