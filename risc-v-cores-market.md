<p><p>RISC-V cores are available in two main market types: RV32 and RV64. RV32 cores are designed for 32-bit applications, while RV64 cores are designed for 64-bit applications. RV32 cores are typically used in smaller, lower-power devices such as microcontrollers and IoT devices, where the focus is on efficiency and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, RV64 cores are commonly used in more demanding applications such as servers and high-performance computing, where the need for 64-bit processing power is essential.</p></p>
