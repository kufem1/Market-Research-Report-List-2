<p><p>Unloader valves are essential components in pressure washer systems, available in two main types: manual and automatic. Manual unloader valves require manual adjustment to regulate pressure, making them more suitable for smaller applications where pressure adjustments are infrequent. On the other hand, automatic unloader valves sense pressure changes and adjust accordingly, making them ideal for continuous or high-volume operations. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and safety of pressure washer systems across various industries.</p></p>
