<p><p>Electric seats in the automotive industry come in two main types - manual adjustment and power adjustment. Manual adjustment seats require the user to physically adjust the seat position using a lever or handle. Power adjustment seats, on the other hand, utilize electric motors to automatically adjust the seat position with the push of a button. While manual adjustment seats are simpler and more cost-effective, power adjustment seats offer more convenience and customization options for users. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
