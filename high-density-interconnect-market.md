<p><p>High Density Interconnect (HDI) technology has three main market types: Single Panel, Double Panel, and Others. Single Panel HDI involves creating high performance circuit boards with intricate designs on a single panel. Double Panel HDI involves layering two circuit boards on top of each other for increased density. The Others market includes innovative HDI solutions beyond traditional single and double panel designs, such as flex-rigid boards or stacked microvias. Each market type caters to different needs for high density and high performance electronic applications.</p></p>
