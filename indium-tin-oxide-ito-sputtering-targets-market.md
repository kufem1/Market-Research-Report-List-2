<p><p>Indium Tin Oxide (ITO) sputtering targets are used in various industries for thin film deposition applications. There are two main types of ITO sputtering targets: Planar targets and Rotary targets. Planar targets are flat and are used in magnetron sputtering systems for uniform film deposition. On the other hand, Rotary targets are cylindrical in shape and are used in rotatable magnetron sputtering systems for higher target material utilization. Both types of targets offer high purity and excellent electrical conductivity for efficient thin film coating processes.</p></p>
