<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Semi Automatic</li><li>Fully Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Degassing valve applicators are available in three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual applicators require operators to manually place and seal the degassing valve onto the packaging. Semi-automatic applicators involve some automation in the process, such as automated valve placement with manual sealing. Fully automatic applicators are completely automated, with the entire valve application process being carried out by machinery. Each type offers varying levels of efficiency and labor savings, catering to different production needs and budgets in the market.</p></p>
